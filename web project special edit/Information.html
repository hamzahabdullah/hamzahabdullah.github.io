The Story Of The Factory And How It Began

Omar Samarah, which is the founder by the way, founded this factory in 1973. The idea got to him because there were no candy factories in that time. So he grouped with some friends of him and told them his idea and the supported him and they started working together.

They started working with little equipment, everything they did was handmade. Then, they started getting bigger and bigger because of the quality they provided which people loved it. So they open this factory to start producing faster and faster.

The factory was originally located in Al Fuhais, Jordan. then it expanded and they created 3 more factroies in three different countries such as: England, Dubai and Egypt.
